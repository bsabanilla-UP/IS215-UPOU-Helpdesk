# UPOU Student Support Services

UPOU provides comprehensive support for dispersed students through various administrative and academic programs.

## Core Support Areas
* **Administrative:** Admission, Registration, Enrollment, and Matriculation.
* **Financial:** Financial assistance and Scholarships.
* **Academic:** Delivery of course materials and Examination setup.
* **Technical:** Guidance on navigating course sites and computer use.

## Digital Learning Tools
### MyPortal (Virtual Classroom)
[cite_start]Powered by Moodle, **MyPortal** is UPOU’s open-source Learning Management System (LMS)[cite: 119].
* **Features:** Discussion boards, real-time chats, announcement repositories, and assignment submission facilities.
* **Function:** Serves as the primary hub for teacher-student interaction and the delivery of "course sites."

### UPOU Networks
An online repository for multimedia scholarly productions produced by the university.
* **UPOU Live:** Web-streamed lectures and university events in real-time.
* **UPOU Commons:** Free repository for Open Educational Resources (OERs).
* **UPOU OnRecord:** Internet radio and podcast repository.
