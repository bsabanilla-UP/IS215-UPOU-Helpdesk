# UPOU Examination Services Program

Managed under the **Office of Student Affairs (OSA)**, this program handles the administration of academic exams (midterms, finals, make-up, and comprehensive).

## Core Functions
* Maintaining the pool of examination venues and proctors.
* Distributing and collecting examination questionnaires.
* Ensuring the integrity and security of all University examinations.

## Contact for Exam Concerns
* **General Support:** support@upou-osa.freshdesk.com
* **Admission Exams (UgAT, GAT):** File a ticket at the [UPOU Helpdesk](https://helpdesk.upou.edu.ph).
* **UPCAT:** Visit [upcat.up.edu.ph](https://upcat.up.edu.ph/).
