## Travel Directions from Metro Manila
### By Private Vehicle
1. Drive southbound via SLEX and take the **Turbina Exit**.
2. Merge onto Pan-Philippine Highway. Turn left onto **Bucal Bypass Rd**, then right onto **Manila S Rd**.
3. Past Olivarez Plaza and Los Baños Junction, turn right to UPOU (across South Supermarket).

### By Public Commute
1. Take a bus traveling to **Sta. Cruz, Laguna**.
2. Inform the conductor your destination is **South Supermarket, Los Baños**.
3. Alight at South Supermarket; UPOU is directly across the highway.
