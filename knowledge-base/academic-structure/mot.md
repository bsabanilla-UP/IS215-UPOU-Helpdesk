# Mode of Teaching at UPOU

The UP Open University (UPOU) utilizes a learner-centered Distance Education (DE) model. Unlike correspondence schools, UPOU provides guided study with support from faculty, tutors, and program advisers.

## Key Features of Distance Education at UPOU
| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Physical Separation** | Students and teachers are physically separated and do not meet in a physical classroom. |
| **Guided Independent Study** | Students use specially designed materials (modules, books, websites, videos) and a **Course Guide** as a roadmap. |
| **Virtual Interaction** | Communication occurs in virtual classrooms, via email, text, or teleconferencing. |
| **Examinations** | Administered through a network of physical venues (local and abroad) or via an online exam system. |

## Learning Support System
* **Faculty & Tutors:** Provide academic guidance for every class.
* **Student Support Staff:** Assist with administrative and learner-specific needs.
* **Program Advisers:** Help students navigate their specific degree paths.
