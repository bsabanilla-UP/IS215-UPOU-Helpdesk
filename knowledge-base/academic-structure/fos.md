# UPOU Faculties of Study (FOS)

UPOU is composed of three main faculties:

| Faculty | Acronym | Specialization / Mandate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Faculty of Education** | FEd | Teacher training, degree, and non-degree education programs. |
| **Faculty of Information and Communication Studies** | FICS | Information and communication studies (Includes MIS). |
| **Faculty of Management and Development Studies** | FMDS | Nursing, health, environment, social work, and public management. |

## Program Statistics
UPOU offers a total of **30 degree programs**, including:
* 2 PhD & 2 Master’s
* 11 Graduate Diplomas & 2 Graduate Certificates
* 3 Undergraduate Programs
* 1 Professional Certification
* 10 Non-formal courses and various MOOCs.
