# Legal Mandates and Philosophy

## Open and Distance eLearning (ODeL)
UPOU utilizes the **ODeL** worldview to promote education equity and access. ODeL combines open learning, distance education, and eLearning with a focus on:
* Learner-centeredness and flexibility
* Active learning and interactivity
* Connectivity and ubiquity

## Republic Act 10650 (Open Distance Learning Act)
UPOU is mandated by **RA 10650** to:
* Upgrade the quality of the Philippine educational system through innovative technologies
* Assist national agencies and other HEIs in developing distance education programs

## Republic Act 11032 (Citizen's Charter)
In compliance with the **Ease of Doing Business Act**, UPOU maintains an official Citizen's Charter for efficient government service delivery.
