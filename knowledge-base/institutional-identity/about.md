# About UP Open University (UPOU)

Established on 23 February 1995, UPOU is the fifth autonomous campus of the UP System and serves as its "Cyber Campus." [cite_start]It pioneers online teaching and distance education (DE) in the Philippines, removing the need for physical proximity through the use of digital media. [cite: 26, 74]

## Quick Facts and Figures
| Category | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Established** | 23 February 1995 |
| **Status** | 5th Autonomous Campus of the UP System |
| **Mandate** | Republic Act 10650 (Open Distance Learning Law) |
| **Learning Mode** | Distance Education (DE) via MyPortal |
| **Programs Offered** | 3 Undergraduate, 13 Post-baccalaureate, 12 Master’s, 2 Doctoral |
| **Global Reach** | Students in more than 70 countries |
| **Infrastructure** | 8 Learning Centers, 20 Testing Centers |

## Learning Model
UPOU utilizes distance education for guided independent study. [cite_start]All learning and teaching interactions occur online via **MyPortal**. [cite: 74, 85]
* [cite_start]**Examinations:** Conducted at Mega Learning Hubs or designated locations. [cite: 26]
* [cite_start]**International Students:** Managed through the Office of Student Affairs Exam Services Program. [cite: 26]
