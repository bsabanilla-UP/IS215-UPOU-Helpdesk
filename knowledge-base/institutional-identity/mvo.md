# UPOU Vision, Mission, and Objectives

[cite_start]The University of the Philippines Open University (UPOU) was established to provide quality and accessible higher education to areas not reached by other UP campuses.

## Vision and Mission
| Type | Official Statement |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vision** | [cite_start]The UP Open University shall be at the forefront of the knowledge society as a leading institution of open learning and distance education. |
| **Mission** | [cite_start]To provide wider access to quality higher education while adhering to academic excellence, guaranteeing academic freedom, and encouraging social responsibility. |

## Institutional Objectives
UPOU is driven by the following three primary goals:
* [cite_start]**Alternative Access:** Provide opportunities for quality higher education through baccalaureate, post-baccalaureate, and non-formal distance education courses.
* [cite_start]**Continuing Education:** Sustain professional growth and technical skills for individuals unable to leave their jobs or homes for full-time study.
* [cite_start]**Educational Upgrading:** Improve the quality of the country's educational system by developing and sharing innovative instructional materials and technology through cooperative programs.

## Headquarters
The UP Open University Headquarters is located in Los Baños, Laguna, Philippines.
